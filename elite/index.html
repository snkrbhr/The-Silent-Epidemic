A System at the Breaking Point

Recent reports from the Ministry of Education and independent mental health surveys indicate a disturbing upward trend in suicide cases across India's premier institutes (IITs, NITs, IIMs, AIIMS) and among top-tier IT professionals. The relentless pursuit of excellence, compounded by isolation, academic rigor, and workplace instability, has created a "pressure cooker" environment. This infographic visualizes the scale of the crisis, breaking down the numbers behind the headlines.
